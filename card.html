Giant Panda

The literal meaning of Panda in Chinese means "bear cat".

  • Scientific Name: Ailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Weight: average 220 - 254lbs
  • Height: 4 - 6 feet
  • Habitat: Native to China and some zoos and breeding centers across the world

The Giant Panda is shaped like a bear and their color is black and white. Only the ears, eye patches, muzzles, legs and shoulders are black on the Giant Panda and the rest is white fur. There is really no scientific information why the Giant Panda's fur is black and white.
